About Yoga Vidya School

Discover the Essence of Yoga at Yoga Vidya School:

Best Yoga School In Rishikesh

Founded by Prashant Jakhamola in 2014, Yoga Vidya School is a place to immerse in serious authentic yoga practice led by the best yoga teachers of India, in an energetic environment created for transformation through Yogic Movements, Mudras, Meditations, Philosophy, and Spiritual Lifestyle. The school emphasizes holistic development of the students’ mental, physical, emotional, spiritual, and professional life. Our curriculum of the courses is based on three pillars, named as Triangular Pyramid Teaching’ - Learn, Experience, and Share.

In Rishikesh, India, the school offers the opportunity to practice this sacred science in the Mother Nature in the foothills of the Himalayas, by the banks of the river Ganges. Here you can experience Indian Yogic Hospitality in a beautiful accommodation facility located in the midst of the Himalayan lush green Rajaji forest and get the delicious Ayurvedic food. At Yoga Vidya School, we also offer Cultural immersions during the retreats and yoga TTC courses through visits to local attractions, Ashrams, Waterfalls, and Trek in the Himalayas.

Outside Rishikesh, Yoga Vidya School is available in Bali, Romania, Russia, and France and offers the courses with translators for a Multilingual experience. In addition to physical presence in the yogic destinations, the school is now a trusted brand in the world for Online Education through Daily Yoga Classes, Yoga TTCs, Specialised Yoga Workshops, and Therapies. These programs are available at multiple timings suitable for worldwide time zones.
