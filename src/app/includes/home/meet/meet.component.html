Acharya Prashant Jakhmola Ji

Introducing Acharya Prashant Jakhmola, an unconventional yet deeply rooted yoga guru and one of the most popular yoga teachers on Youtube. As the founder of Yoga Vidya School in Rishikesh, India, Acharya Prashant embodies the timeless wisdom of traditional yoga while embracing innovative approaches to practice and teaching. With easy and detail explanations, Prashant’s teaching techniques focus on fostering a profound connection with the body and spirit guiding his students towards the path of correct authentic practices and positive mind body transformation. The bond with his students is evident from his quality teachings and clear communication with the students, ensuring deep attention to the needs of each student.

The Acharya meticulously imparts ancient teachings with a modern perspective, ensuring their relevance and applicability in contemporary life. Acharya Prashant's dedication to preserving traditional wisdom is evident in his reverence for yoga philosophy, meditation, and the teachings of Maharishi Patanjali’s yoga sutras and Bhagavad Gita. Through his nurturing guidance, he inspires students to delve deeper into the essence of yoga, ostering personal health and spiritual evolution
