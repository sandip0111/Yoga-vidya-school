Why Rishikesh?

Rishikesh, nestled at the foothills of the Himalayas and along the sacred Ganges River, is the spiritual heart of yoga.

This ancient town is home to countless sages, yogis, and seekers, and offers the ideal environment to dive into the true essence of yoga.
