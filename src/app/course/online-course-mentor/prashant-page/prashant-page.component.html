Online Sadhana - By Prashant Jakhmola

Holistic Health

Prashant´s online yoga classes are not only designed to offer physical benefits such as flexibility, strong and active body or appearance. These classes work on deeper level for holistic health, with combination of three major areas of human life:

  • Physical
  • Mental
  • Spiritual

Yoga is a practice that deeply impacts all those levels of life which are very essential to a living person. It is not the practice of the body only. It is a process of spiritual development. And keeping in mind all these important things, we have conducted yoga classes. These classes are conducted online to all yoga aspirants who want to bring spiritual transformation in their life through yoga.

Practicing live with me is highly beneficial especially if anyone wants to develop a regular routine and practice.

What will you learn with Prashant?

  • Proper way of practising Asana and Pranayama
  • Alignment of each asana practice
  • Modification of practice according to individual limitations and strengths
  • Maximum Individual Attention to each student
  • Shatkarma (yogic purification) such as neti practice
  • Reach a new level in your personal yoga and meditation practice
