Ashtanga & Alignment-Based Practice - By Pankaj

Strength & Structure

This practice combines the flowing rhythm of Ashtanga Vinyasa with a strong focus on alignment and mindful adjustments. Each asana is explored with practical details, helping students understand correct posture, breath, and movement. Through clear guidance and awareness, the practice builds strength, stability, flexibility, and a deeper connection to the body supporting safe, sustainable, and conscious yoga practice.

Aligned Practice

Ashtanga classes on Mondays and Wednesdays cultivate strength, continuity, and discipline, while alignment-based hatha sessions on Tuesdays and Thursdays focus on stability, and refined awareness. A complete and balanced path for those seeking both fire and structure in their practice.
