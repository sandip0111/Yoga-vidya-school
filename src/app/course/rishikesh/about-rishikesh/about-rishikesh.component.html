Deeper Your Yoga Practice and Advance Your Yoga Teachings

If you’ve already completed your 200-Hour Yoga Teacher Training, attended workshops, or studied with other schools, our 300-Hour Advanced Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh is your next step.

This immersive program will refine your alignment, deepen your understanding of yoga philosophy, and elevate your teaching skills so you can guide students with confidence, clarity, and authenticity.

We meet you exactly where you are and help you grow beyond your current limits—whether that means developing a stronger personal practice, mastering advanced asanas, or refining the way you structure and deliver your classes.

At Yoga Vidya School, we believe your teaching is a reflection of your inner practice and the way you live yoga on and off the mat. Our 300-hour training will empower you to embody the qualities of a dedicated practitioner and a truly inspiring teacher.

Who This Training is For

Our 300-Hour YTT is designed for yoga practitioners and teachers who:
  • 1. Have completed a 200-Hour YTT and wish to gain deeper knowledge of yoga science.
  • 2. Want to expand their understanding of Yoga Philosophy, Anatomy, and Biomechanics.
  • 3. Are ready to explore advanced asanas, pranayama, meditation, and yoga nidra in depth.
  • 4. Seek to build more confidence, presence, and skill in teaching yoga.
  • 5. Aim to lead intermediate and advanced-level classes with ease.
  • 6. Are on the path to becoming RYT-500 certified and committed to sharing yoga with authenticity and heart.
