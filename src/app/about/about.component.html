Why Yoga Vidya School?

The above Shloka explains the true meaning of ‘Vidya,’ a Sanskrit word for Knowledge. The knowledge of Yoga gives inner peace and liberty and thats what inspired us to create Yoga Vidya School – the place of peace and liberty with meaningful practice of Yoga.

Yoga Vidya School is the traditional Yoga And Vedanta school based in Rishikesh, follows two Yogic traditions:

  • Swami Satyananda Saraswati (Bihar School of Yoga)
  • T Krishnamacharya and K Pattabhi Jois (Ashtanga Vinyasa)

The theoretical subjects are inspired and based on Vedantic philosophy by Shankaracharya Vedanta, in addition, we cover philosophies of Bhagavad Gita, Patanjali Yoga Sutra, Hatha Pradipika, Upanishads, etc.

Our yoga training modules let the students gain the practical and philosophical knowledge of Hatha Yoga and Ashtanga Vinyasa both.

The Paths of Learning We Share

Our school emphasise on all round development of the students’ life. So we based our teaching process on three pillars, named as “triangular pyramid teaching”

These pillars are:  Learn, Experience and Share.

Every student of the Yoga Vidya community has to step seriously into all these pillars to become a dedicated Practitioner and experienced Teacher.

Whether you are an intermediate yoga practitioner or an experienced yoga instructor looking to master the art of teaching advanced yoga postures or a beginner Yoga enthusiast, We cater all your requirements.

Yoga Alliance Certified 100 Hour, 200 Hour, 300 Hour and 500 Hour Yoga Teacher Training, Pranayama Therapy, Specialisation of Alignment & Adjustments of Asana, Breath Detox, Chakra Marathon, Yoga Darshan, Pranayama Sadhna, Ashtanga Sadhana and more...

Our Mission in all Yoga Destinations

Our mission is to bring out the ancient practices of yoga from the Upanishads and the Shastras into the World. So the practitioners can practice these hidden techniques to uplift their lifestyle. A lifestyle which is described in the ancient Yoga Scriptures, A way of living to achieve holistic health and happiness, balancing the physical, mental and spiritual development. To spread this knowledge, we work on creating dedicated yoga teachers through various yoga courses and events. The experiences and the knowledge of our yoga guru’s are conveyed through these yoga courses to yoga sadhaka(the practitioners), and so the yoga exercises are learned and conveyed to others in its authentic form by yoga practitioners.
